About Us

Our Mission

PolluGuard was founded with a vision to restore the once vibrant ecosystem of the Niger Delta. Decades of oil exploration have significantly polluted the air, water, and soil, leaving the local communities vulnerable and the natural habitat in distress. Our mission is to combat this environmental injustice through robust, data-driven advocacy and community engagement.

Who We Are

PolluGuard is more than just a website. We are a beacon of hope for the Niger Delta, committed to providing the community with crucial air quality data and weather forecasts that go beyond the generic reports. Through our platform, we expose the harsh realities of environmental degradation in the region and mobilize action towards sustainable solutions.
