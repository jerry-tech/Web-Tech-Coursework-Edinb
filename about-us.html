About Us

A brief history about us and why we do what we do.

The Nigerian Niger Delta, once a vibrant tapestry of life, now struggles to breathe. Decades of oil exploration have choked its air with pollutants, leaving millions gasping for clean air and a healthy future. Polluguard emerges not just as a website, but also as a beacon of hope in this fight for environmental justice. Forget the generic weather reports. Polluguard dives deeper, providing crucial air quality data, exposing the invisible enemy stealing the breath from the Niger Delta. It unveils the grim reality through compelling narratives and heartbreaking images of affected communities, shattering the silence surrounding the region's plight.
